About Us

At Dhyana Yogashala, we are dedicated to spreading the transformative power of yoga. Nestled in the serene foothills of Rishikesh, our studio offers a sanctuary for self-discovery and growth. Our experienced teachers guide you through a holistic practice rooted in Hatha, Vinyasa, Iyengar, and Ashtanga traditions, fostering wellness for body, mind, and spirit. Join us to embark on a journey of balance and inner peace.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves Flexibility

    Regular practice increases your range of motion and reduces stiffness.

  • Reduces Stress

    Yoga promotes relaxation and lowers cortisol levels.

  • Boosts Strength

    Poses build muscle endurance and core stability.

  • Enhances Mental Clarity

    Mindfulness and breathing exercises sharpen focus.
