Our Story

Hi there! We're Dhyana Yogashala, and we're passionate about sharing the incredible benefits of yoga with everyone. Located in the peaceful foothills of Rishikesh (the yoga capital of the world!), our studio is more than just a place to practice yoga it's a community where you can discover yourself, make lasting friendships, and learn from experienced teachers who truly care about your growth.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, our warm and welcoming environment will make you feel right at home. We believe yoga is for everyone, and we're here to guide you every step of the way on your journey to wellness and inner peace.

Why Yoga Changes Everything

You've probably heard that yoga is good for you, but let us tell you exactly why it's such a game-changer:

  • Feel More Flexible (Really!)

    Remember when you couldn't touch your toes? After just a few weeks of yoga, you'll be amazed at what your body can do. It's like your muscles finally remember how to move!

  • Say Goodbye to Stress

    Life can be overwhelming, right? Yoga teaches you how to breathe through the chaos and find your calm center. It's like having a reset button for your mind.

  • Get Stronger (Without the Gym)

    You'll build real strength by holding poses and moving your body in new ways. Plus, you'll feel more confident in everything you do.

  • Clear Your Mind

    In our busy world, finding mental clarity is priceless. Yoga helps you focus, make better decisions, and feel more present in your daily life.

200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training

Perfect for Beginners

This is where your teaching journey begins! Our 200-hour program is designed for anyone who wants to deepen their practice and learn how to share yoga with others. You don't need to be super flexible or have years of experience just bring your enthusiasm and open mind!

  • Duration: 28 Days / 27 Nights (perfect for a month-long adventure!)
  • Certification: Yoga Alliance, USA (recognized worldwide)
  • What You'll Learn: Hatha, Vinyasa, Iyengar, and Ashtanga styles
  • Perfect For: Beginners, aspiring teachers, and anyone wanting to deepen their practice
