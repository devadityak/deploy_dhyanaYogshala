What We Teach

Asana Practice & Alignment

Master the art of yoga postures with proper alignment, modifications, and adjustments for different body types.

Pranayama & Breathing

Learn ancient breathing techniques to control life force energy and enhance mental clarity and focus.

Meditation & Mindfulness

Develop deep meditation practices and mindfulness techniques for inner peace and spiritual growth.

Yoga Philosophy

Study ancient texts like Patanjali's Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita, and traditional yoga philosophy.

Anatomy & Physiology

Understand human anatomy in relation to yoga practice for safe and effective teaching.

Teaching Methodology

Learn effective teaching techniques, class sequencing, and how to create transformative yoga experiences.
