About Dhyana Yogashala

Discover the heart and soul of our yoga community, where ancient wisdom meets modern practice in the sacred land of Rishikesh.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to spread the authentic teachings of yoga, Dhyana Yogashala has been a beacon of spiritual growth and transformation since its inception.

Our journey began in the sacred city of Rishikesh, where the ancient wisdom of yoga has been preserved for centuries. We believe in making this profound knowledge accessible to seekers from around the world, regardless of their background or experience level.

At Dhyana Yogashala, we don't just teach yoga poses; we guide you through a complete transformation of body, mind, and spirit. Our approach combines traditional Hatha, Vinyasa, and Ashtanga practices with modern understanding of anatomy and physiology.

Our Sacred Location

Nestled in the foothills of the majestic Himalayas, our yoga studio is located in the spiritual capital of the world - Rishikesh.

Rishikesh, often called the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a sacred city where the holy Ganges River flows from the mountains to the plains. This divine location provides the perfect environment for spiritual growth and yoga practice.

Our studio is surrounded by lush greenery, with the sound of flowing water and chanting creating a serene atmosphere that enhances your yoga journey. The energy of this sacred place amplifies the transformative power of your practice.
